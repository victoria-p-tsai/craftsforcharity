Drop Off Crafts or Donated Supplies

Mailing Location

Please mail all donated materials or crafts to 7445 Cherry Tree Dr Fulton, MD, 20759.

Drop Off Boxes

We currently have a drop-off box located at Happy Helpers for the Homeless (1550 Caton Center Drive Halethorpe, MD 21227) if you would like to drop-off items in person.

Steps to Drop Off

First, take all of your crafts or donated materials, and put them in a closed plastic bag labeled "CRAFTS FOR CHARITY". Make sure that they are as closed off to the environment as possible.

Next, fill out, submit, and print the form below with what you are bringing. If you do not have ability to print the form, submit the form digitally and then enclose a paper with your name and email with your donations. Make sure the form is inside your bag before you drop it off, and you are all set!

Thank you for all of your time and help with our mission. If you are interested in joining our team, see our “Volunteer” tab for more information on how to apply. Check out our “Who We Support” page under our “About Us” tab to see why we do what we do!

Service Hours

Crafts for Charity will grant 3 service hours per rectangle donated and 6 service hours per scarf; however, if you spent more time knitting/crocheting your items, please let us know when requesting confirmation of your hours! Confirmation will be granted and documentation will be signed once a CFC has received your donation.
