MEMBER POSITIONS

Prospective volunteers of Crafts for Charity may apply for multiple teams across three general departments: Logistics, Outreach, and Teaching. Each team has different tasks and responsibilities, which you may learn more about down below.

Volunteers are essential to Crafts for Charity and we appreciate each and every volunteer that works for us! If you are interested in volunteering, please read through the team descriptions, the general requirements, and then fill out an application here:

MEMBER BENEFITS

  • Earn service hours while helping your community and local charities
  • Gain valuable experience that can be used on resumes for future jobs, internships, or college applications
  • Build stronger interpersonal and technical skills

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

To volunteer, you must be between 14-17 years of age. If accepted, you must be committed to volunteer for at least 2 months.

DESIRABLE QUALIFICATIONS

Teams have more specific qualifications, but in general, we look for these attributes in an applicant:

  • Strong communication and writing skills
  • Ability to collaborate effectively in teams

LOGISTICS

Teams in the logistics department work together to ensure that all operations in CFC are working effectively and smoothly.

OUTREACH

Teams in the Outreach department are responsible for promoting CFC and expanding our connections with various groups and members of our community.

TEACHING

Our teachers work together to provide free virtual craft instruction to students of all ages and skill levels. Currently, we offer classes in knitting and crocheting.
